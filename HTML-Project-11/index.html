Volunteer Experience

Open Heart Kitchen

2016-2019

Open Heart Kitchen is a volunteer service that helps the community with free food services. It was a very fun experience as helping people with smiles and seeing them happy to eat food or take boxes to feed their family. All of them are very thankful for this service as it is cheap, sanitary, and helps a lot of people that are in a financial crisis. It really reflects how blessed we are to be able to have many material possessions while many others have just barely enough to provide for their families.

Scratch Project

CSP

I was in a group of two where we had to create a story or game in a programming website called Scratch. With some brainstorming along with influence of playing mobile games in the past, we created a car game. In the start of the game, the player robs a bank and gets inside a getaway car in which the player has to maneuver the car away from obstacles on the side of the road as well as the middle of the road with an additional police car ready to capture at any moment.
